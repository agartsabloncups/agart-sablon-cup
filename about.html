Crafting Quality, Shaping Identity About AG ART

Since its establishment in 2015, AG ART has been a trusted cup printing house known for precise results, attractive designs, and excellent service. With extensive experience serving various market segments, around 80 percent of our clients come from coffee shops in Tasikmalaya, while the remaining 20 percent are from outside the city who trust us to represent their brand identity. We believe that packaging is more than just product protection because it reflects the character of a brand and enhances the value of every product.

Through friendly service, an easy process, and transparent communication, AG ART is committed to delivering results that truly meet customer expectations. We also provide various cup brands such as Starindo, SAP, BSM, Ecoplas, and others , helping clients find the best options that match their brand concept and business needs. With dedication to quality and customer satisfaction, we continue to build loyalty and trust in every print we deliver.
